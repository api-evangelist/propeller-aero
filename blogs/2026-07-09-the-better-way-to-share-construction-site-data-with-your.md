Sharing construction site data still happens over email chains, USB drives, and screenshots in group texts for a lot of teams, and that leaves everyone working from different versions of the site. This guide breaks down where those common sharing methods fall short, what good actually looks like, and how one shared map keeps the field, the office, and ownership aligned on the same current view. The payoff is simple: less rework, faster decisions, and site data everyone can access and actually understand.
